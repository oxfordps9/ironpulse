Fuel Your Body

Healthy Nutrition & Daily Routine

Exercise is only 30% of the journey; the remaining 70% relies entirely on your eating habits, hydration, and recovery discipline.

Clean Eating Habits

Prioritize natural, unprocessed whole foods. Fill your meals with lean proteins (chicken, eggs, beef), complex carbohydrates (oats, sweet potatoes, brown rice), and fresh greens to properly fuel trained muscle fibers.

Hydration & Routine

Drink 3-4 liters of water daily to maintain performance. Keep a strict timing schedule: consume clean carbs pre-workout for energy, and premium protein within 45 minutes post-workout for fast recovery.

Rest Protocol

Muscles do not grow while you lift weights on the gym floor; they grow when you rest. Ensure 7-8 hours of deep sleep to lower cortisol stress levels and allow perfect tissue rebuilding.
